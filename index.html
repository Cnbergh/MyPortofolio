Who am I

My name is Christian and I am a front-end student at Noroff.
Having completed my first year, I have gained substantial knowledge in UI/UX design, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
I find these areas extremely fascinating and I am eager to continue learning more, and tackle new challenges.
I am passionate about applying the skills I have learned to practical problems and finding innovative solutions.

NO 1 Cross-course project +

RainyDays marked the beginning of my project journey, during which I created an online storefront for a brand specializing in outdoor jackets for both men and women. The primary goal of the website was to resonate with its intended audience, specifically individuals aged between 30 to 50.

NO 2 Semester Project 1 +

This was my culminating project for the first semester, where I was tasked to design and develop a website for the fictional "Community Science Museum". The project was first designed in Figma and subsequently coded strictly with HTML and CSS. The main focus of the website was to appeal to its target audience, specifically family's with children aged between 7 to 15.

NO 3 Project Exam 1 +

This exam presented an opportunity to practically apply the skills we'd amassed over our first year of study. The task was to design and develop a functional blog site, complete with specific pages and features. The goal was not just to test our understanding of the foundational elements but also to demonstrate our ability to create a cohesive and user-friendly digital platform.
